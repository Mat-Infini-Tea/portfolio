Introduction


Over the past few years, wildfires have been getting increasing attention worldwide for how much more frequently they have been occurring, combined with their ever-growing scale. Due to climate change these catastrophic events are predicted to become even worse. And due to the highly unpredictable and destructive nature of wildfires combatting them is incredibly challenging. But one potential way we can attempt to limit their damage is through better planning of settlements and infrastructure. Yet there is a lack of research that has been taken into the settlement planning, such as 3D visualisations of plans with a focus on defence from wildfires, even though they are becoming increasingly prevalent and damaging. The aim of this project is to research the best practices of 3D digital representations of settlement planning in response to wildfire risk, and then implement them in a showcase model, that also highlights the various uncertainties associated.
